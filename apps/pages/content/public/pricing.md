# Rowset pricing

Hosted Rowset includes full product access for 7 days, then costs $50 per month. Connect an agent,
create real datasets, and test the workflow with no credit card required. You can cancel at any
time and export your data. Prefer to operate it yourself? Rowset is open source and self-hostable.

## 7-day trial — $0

Use the complete product to connect an agent, create real datasets, and confirm Rowset fits your
workflow. The trial includes:

- Hosted MCP, REST API, and CLI access
- Private datasets and rows
- Typed columns and stable indexes
- Semantic search
- CSV, JSONL, XLSX, SQLite, and Parquet exports
- Optional read-only public previews

[Start your 7-day trial]({{ signup_url }}).

## Pro — $50 per month

Pro is for ongoing agent workflows across research, operations, content, customer data, and other
structured work. It includes everything in the trial, unlimited hosted datasets, unlimited rows,
multi-dataset projects, and one predictable monthly bill as usage grows.

## What stays the same

- **Private by default:** bearer-key access stays within your account boundary.
- **Agent-native:** use MCP first, with REST and CLI available for code and other clients.
- **Portable:** export your datasets in five common formats whenever you need them.
- **Human-readable:** inspect results and deliberately enable read-only public previews.

The repository includes deployment paths for teams that want to self-host Rowset.
